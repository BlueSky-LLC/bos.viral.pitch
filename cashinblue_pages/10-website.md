## What can AI Website do?

### Triggers:

#### Time & Date

Create a date and time trigger once and use it continuously by setting it up to reoccur at any time, on any date you want it, daily, weekly or monthly.

#### Number of Visits

A first-time visitor is most likely looking for something different than a returning customer. Easily determine what visitors see based on how often they have previously engaged with your site.

#### Device Type

Craft specialised calls-to-actions, messages and layouts that best match the device your visitor is using.

#### Location

Use geo-targeting to ensure your site visitors are seeing content that is relevant to their physical location.

#### Campaign URL

Running an ad campaign? Create a unique link with the Campaign URL trigger. Once clicked, this link will take your visitor to your site, but show them an experience that directly relates to your ad.

#### Seasonal Personalisation

Personalise your website at different seasons and events such as Christmas, Halloween, Valentines, Easter and so much more!

### Actions

#### Display New Content

Show a new section on a page when this action is triggered. You can add any type of content, show a coupon, display a special message or a contact form, play a video and more.

#### Show Notification Bar

Run a message across the top of your website to highlight a promotion, or deliver a special message to your visitors.

#### Show Popup

Show a popup on any selected page. You can add a popup with a message to a first-time visitor, location map to a nearby visitor, show a contact form for off-hours visitors, capture visitor's attention with a special promotion and more.

#### Display Special Effects

Make your website even more dynamic with special effects. You can choose to show falling snowflakes, leaves, hearts, halloween treats, popping fireworks, stars and more.
